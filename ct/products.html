- MILITARY AEROSPACE


Cen Techno Craft Company combines decades of experience with industry leading supply chain management support to delivery superior service. As an authorized supplier for a variety of hardware, chemicals, electronics and other consumable items and spare parts, Cen Techno Craft Company has provided support for fixed wing and rotary wing platforms around the world.

In support of Operations Enduring Freedom and Operation Iraqi Freedom, Cen Techno Craft Company has been a key supplier for the United States Department of Defense, along with numerous prime and sub-contractors. With direct program experience in support of Bell and Sikorsky Helicopter platforms, Cen Techno Craft Company is a leading expert in military helicopter support. We support from engine components to rotor blades, and from consumable hardware to avionics, Cen Techno Craft Company has the ability to support all rotary wing platforms currently in service with the United States and its allies. Cen Techno Craft Company has maintained an impeccable record of quality and service in all activities.

CTC has worked closely with prime contractors in support of the C-130, F-5, Fokker, F-16, F/A-18, F-35, Tucano and numerous other fighter and attack aircraft in service with the Military. These efforts have included production support, maintenance and repair activities, and overhaul support. In addition to support of the full range of Military Aircraft, CTC has worked closely in support of allied nations in the maintenance and service of their warfighting machines. This effort has included full Global Trade Compliance, and CTC technology maintains the know-how and ability to ship product around the world while in full compliance with US and International Trade Compliance Regulations.


- ELECTRONICS


With decades of experience in the ever changing electronics field, Cen Techno Craft Company has the capability and personnel to provide a diverse selection of electronics parts and components. Our company has long served the military and commercial electronics markets.

Cen Techno Craft Company recognizes and thrives on relationships built by trust and integrity. Long-standing relationships with franchised distributors and manufacturers allow us to offer nothing but authentic parts and components to our valued clients. A fully staffed and capably trained team of inspectors, guided by the standards of both ANSI/ESD 20.20 and IDEA, ensures that only authentic parts reach our customers.

Our customer service representatives are on call and accessible around the clock, and are committed to providing the quickest and best customer service in the industry. Combined with years of experience, and unparalleled product knowledge, our team of electronics experts come to work every day to serve you, our customer.
