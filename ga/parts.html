Parts Supply & Services

Market leader in component repair and supply of top quality spare parts for rotary aircraft, Global Aeronautics supports the best helicopters in the world, including several high-speed models manufactured by MD Helicopters, Inc. All have single turbine Rolls-Royce engines and five-blade rotor systems. These are the MD500, MD520, and MD530.
  • The best performing helicopter in its class, the MD500 is derived from OH-6A observation helicopters used by the US army during the Vietnam conflict. Its design is considered highly crashworthy.
  • The MD 520 that is used for a wide range of mission applications from fire fighting, air rescue, and paramilitary, to agricultural and forestry, aerial survey and photographic assignments. It is one of the safest, quietest helicopters available.
  • The high performance, multi-mission capable MD 530 used globally by government, foreign military, and commercial operators.
