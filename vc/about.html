About us

Vesuki Corp is a Global Solution Provider for the support and sustainment of strategic aerospace assets.


Vesuki Corp supports this corporate vision through Global Engineering, Procurement, specializing in the Support of U.S. Manufactured Military & Aerospace equipment & Provisions.Vesuki Corp provides Spare Parts, repair and overhaul to air force foreign and Domestic, Governments and other operators. Our customers call on us for simple parts to complex assemblies.

We provide Quality Control, Assembly and Testing, Parts Supply, Packaging and Shipping from our dock to yours. We are proudly serving our customers with on-time delivery and superior quality.
