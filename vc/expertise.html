We also support of the deHavilland family of Aircraft our priority, including the DHC-2 Beaver, DHC-3 Otter, DHC-4 Caribou, DHC-5 Buffalo, DHC-6 Twin Otter and the DASH-7.


We carry vast inventories of airframe, rotables and consumables for all the above aircraft. Whether a customer requires a complete wing or just some nuts and bolts we endeavor to have the parts on the shelf ready for delivery.

Vesuki Corp not only maintains inventories of deHavilland manufactured parts, but we also make enormous efforts to stock the many other vendor spares which are utilized in the deHavilland aircraft, including but not limited to Heroux, Pratt & Whitney, Adams Rite Sabre Industries, Goodyear, Goodrich, Cleveland, Allied Signal, Parker Hannifin, Dowty and Plessey, just to name a few.

Most items are readily available and can be quickly obtained by contacting us. In the rare case where an item is not readily available, Vesuki Corp is committed to making every effort possible to find it for you. Over the years our customers have come to rely on Vesuki Corp for quality items, quick delivery and competitive prices. Most of all, though, they count on Vesuki Corp for personalized service.

We provide that service through our different departments: Sales, Purchasing, Shipping & Receiving, Warehouse and Quality Control… each with its clearly defined responsibilities, but all structured to work together to provide our customers with the prompt, efficient and courteous service they have come to expect, no matter how big or small the company, or the request.
