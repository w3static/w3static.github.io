About us

As a privately held aerospace company, Avial International Inc provides industry-leading supply, certified solutions for the helicopter and aerospace industry.

Avial International Inc offers a comprehensive portfolio of aftermarket products, aeronautical accessories, spare parts and services for civil and military operators, all major rotorcraft OEMs, completion centers and MRO facilities.

Avial's key products include: emergency flotation systems, landing gear components, tools and ground support equipment, interior and exterior accessories, cargo expansion, windows and doors, cable cutters, engine and drive systems and external load equipment.

Operational excellence and an unmatched safety record are our hallmarks. We are much more than a commercial air service provider, we are a repository of premium intellectual capital in tactical aviation and related defense and security fields.

Avial International Inc is proud to offer the highest value, quality and durability available.
